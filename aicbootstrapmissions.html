Feeding Program

The feeding program is a project that provides both educational and health benefits to vulnerable children.
It promotes the development and health of children
It improves concentration and academic performance in school
It supports the children’s parents and guardians
It promotes farmers and local vendors
It assists the community rise above poverty
Basically, the programs help to keep the children in school hence complete their education.

Orphanage

Hopez Ministries Children’s Orphanage started by accommodating 10 children, but over the years the number of children increased, and now has over 200orphans, both girls and boys. The children are between 0 and 17 years old. The Orphanage gives hope to the less fortunate children by providing them with a warm homely environment. The Orphanage has a big room for a soccer field, volleyball field, and a basketball court. Also, we music classes for talented children on singing and playing musical instruments who then take part in praise and worship sessions in our church.

Nursing Home

Our nursing home provides care and support for the elderly so that they are able to manage their daily life.

OUR PACKAGE CONTAINS:

  1. Personal Care
  2. Feeding
  3. Showering & dressing
  4. Housework & laundry
  5. Nursing Care
  6. Allied Health services,physio & podiatry

Guidance & Counselling
